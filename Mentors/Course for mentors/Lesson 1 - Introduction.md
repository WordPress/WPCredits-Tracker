# Introduction

Welcome to the WordPress Credits Mentor Course\!

In this program, you’ll learn how to effectively guide students through their contribution projects in the WordPress Credits program.

As a mentor, **you play a pivotal role in shaping students’ first experience** in an open-source ecosystem. You’re not just supervising tasks; you’re helping learners discover their strengths, develop real-world skills, and become confident contributors to the WordPress community.

This course will give you a clear understanding of:

* What’s expected from you as a mentor  
* How to support and track your students’ progress  
* How to connect students with the right WordPress teams and resources  
* How to handle common challenges during mentorship

By the end of this course, you’ll be equipped with the tools, structure, and mindset to make your mentorship meaningful, impactful, and enjoyable.
