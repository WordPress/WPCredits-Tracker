# Institutions Onboarding

Most steps depend on the status of the Institution in Airtable 'Institutions (Form)' and 'Institution onboarding' tables, so they are activated when this status changes.

## Step 1: When a Form of Interest is Sent

1. The institution fill up the form from 'Institutions (Form)' table.
2. The form sends automatically an email to intership@wordpressfundation.org.
3. Airtable sends an email to asking the student to book a meeting or continue as discussed during the previous call (see [this template](https://secure.helpscout.net/settings/inbox/348355/saved-replies/3950163) as reference).
