## **Strategy and Execution Plan**

The core strategy is to establish **WordPress Credits as a scalable, global program** that consistently generates new contributors, broadens access, and increases project health. We will achieve this by combining focused outreach, high quality content, cross-team integration, and continuous iteration based on measurable outcomes.

This strategy focuses on three pillars: **growth through partnerships**, **visibility through storytelling**, and **impact through structure**.

### **2025 – Foundation and Visibility**

**Goals:**

* Solidify program infrastructure  
* Test outreach and onboarding systems  
* Build foundational partnerships  
* Generate early visibility

**Key Tasks:**

* Reach out to **at least 20 universities** across **10 countries**, targeting those with digital media, software engineering, or humanities programs  
* Establish **4 confirmed university partnerships** for 2026 cycles  
* Finalize intern and mentor handbooks, application templates, evaluation systems, and reporting dashboards  
* Reach out to **15 media platforms** (blogs, podcasts, newsletters, educational press)  
* Secure **5 media mentions or features** about WordPress Credits  
* Publish at least **3 stories** of current or past participants on WordPress.org and social media  
* Engage at least **2 Five for the Future sponsored contributors** as mentors or program coordinators  
* Share results and recommendations from the 2025 pilot cohort with relevant contributor teams and executive leadership

### **2026 – Scale and Strengthen**

**Goals:**

* Expand institutional and contributor area participation  
* Grow the number of interns  
* Track and publish measurable outcomes

**Key Tasks:**

* Reach out to **50 additional universities** across **25 countries**  
* Confirm **10 institutional partners** running WordPress Credits annually  
* Train and onboard **30 to 50 student contributors** across **6 or more contribution areas**  
* Build internal pipeline for contribution credits, badges, or university recognition  
* Grow outreach efforts with **monthly content** on WordPress.org, social, and strategic media  
* Secure **10+ media stories or mentions** globally  
* Launch a **global mentors cohort** including returning contributors and company representatives  
* Expand Interns scholarships to **all flagship and regional WordCamps**, with clear post-event engagement paths  
* Create and share an **annual impact report**, highlighting intern retention, team feedback, and scholarship recipient outcomes

### **2027 – Consolidate and Expand**

**Goals:**

* Make WordPress Credits a globally recognized program  
* Strengthen alumni networks and post-program engagement  
* Scale contributor onboarding across language and region

**Key Tasks:**

* Expand university outreach to **100+ institutions** across **40 countries**  
* Translate onboarding materials into **all languages** where the WordPress Credits program is launched  
* Maintain ongoing relationships with **20 university partners**  
* Place **50+ student contributors** across contribution areas per year  
* Launch a **global alumni network**, with storytelling, professional pathways, and mentorship opportunities  
* Track **contributor retention at 6 and 12 months** post-program  
* Secure connection for **sponsorship or hiring partnerships** with **3+ Five for the Future companies**  
* Share program methodology and open templates with the broader open source ecosystem

### **Planning Areas**

#### **1\. Contribution Pathways & Distribution**

* Local VS global  
* Coordinate with team reps and project leads  
* Ideal to have an dashboard for teams/projects backlog  
* Use above data to pace onboarding waves.

#### **3\. Mentorship Pool**

* Grow a mentor pool from trusted contributors and alumni.  
* Define mentor-student ratios to ensure support and reduce burnout.  
* Provide mentors with onboarding guides and optional stipends or recognition (via Five for the Future).

#### **4\. Centralized Admin & Coordination**

* Maintain a small admin team to:  
  * Coordinate placements  
  * Track progress  
  * Intervene in case of blockers  
* Automate reminders, milestone tracking, and reports.

#### **6\. Feedback Loops**

* After each round, gather structured feedback from:  
  * Contributors  
  * Mentors  
  * Team reps

* Use it to adjust future cycles, team distribution, and mentor recruitment.

#### **7\. Off-Ramping Options**

* Help students transition to long-term contribution paths:  
  * Invite them to continue as volunteers  
  * Share 5ftF-funded roles or internships  
  * Match with companies seeking junior talent
