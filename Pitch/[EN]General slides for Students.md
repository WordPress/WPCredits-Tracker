# WordPress Credits

## **A bridge to open-source technology**

The backbone of WordPress educational initiatives like WordPress Credits is the philosophy of open source - keeping the web open, accessible, and built by everyone. This is important because an open web ensures that no single company or institution controls the tools we use to learn, create, and communicate.

---

## **What is open-source?**

Open source empowers students to understand how technology works, contribute to real projects, and shape the future rather than just consume it. It builds digital independence, encourages collaboration, and teaches the value of sharing knowledge.

### **Why does it matter?**

By participating in the WordPress Credits programs, as student, you aren't just learning WordPress - you're becoming part of a global community working together to keep the internet free, innovative, and available to all.

---

## **Why WordPress?**

WordPress is the world's most popular platform for creating websites - free, open source, and easy to use. It gives you the opportunity to develop digital, project management, and communication skills highly valued across industries.

### 43% World Websites

### \+100 Countries

### Easy entry into the tech world

WordPress gives you practical, in-demand abilities that employers look for - from building websites to understanding digital tools. You can start creating projects quickly, even without programming experience.
