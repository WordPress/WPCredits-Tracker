# WordPress Credits

## **A bridge to open-source technology**

An innovative educational contribution program by the WordPress Foundation that brings students into the heart of the WordPress ecosystem so they can gain real-world experience while contributing to a global open-source project.

---

## **What is the WordPress Foundation?**

### **Charitable foundation**

Founded by Matt Mullenweg, co-founder of WordPress, this independent nonprofit stewards the WordPress open-source project for the benefit of all users.

### Global mission

Its objective is to democratize publishing and promote equitable access to the web through open-source software.

### Education and research

WPF supports education, research, and community initiatives without discrimination, ensuring global participation and inclusion.

## **Program Pathway**

This is a journey that transforms students into active contributors. Each stage builds concrete skills and lasting professional connections.

1. ### Onboarding

Introduction to the WordPress ecosystem and its global community.

2. ### Mentorship

Connection with mentor who will accompany you on your journey.

3. ### Project Design

Designing a personal project based on your interests.

4. ### Site creation

Development of your personal website with WordPress.

5. ### Contribution

Work on the project and share progress on your site.

6. ### Continuity

Site ownership and ability to remain in the community.
